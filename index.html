THE NEW EAU DE TOILETTE BY YVES SAINT LAURENT

The fragrance of a sensual & irreverent woman unveiling a new undressed freedom, the freedom to live everything on the skin.

Created by the master perfumers Anne Flipo & Carlos Benaim to unveil a new sensual & undressed freedom, The freedom to live everything on the skin. The iconic structure of LIBRE, a tension between noble orange blossom from Morocco and sustainably-sourced lavender from France, exclusively crafted for Yves Saint Laurent, is reinterpreted in a modern, bright and fresh sensuality.

The carnal femininity of orange blossom absolute is infused with a pure white tea accord, exacerbating the warm-cold duality of the fragrance to leave a shivering yet sun-kissed sensuous pleasure.

The iconic LIBRE IT-bottle is reinvented in a new elongated shape whose vertical extension clashes with the couture extravagance of the gold oversize horizontal Cassandre bent & nailed into the glass.

Libre Eau de Toilette encapsulates a new delicate golden pink fragrance shade, celebrating femininity on the edge of nude. The embodiment of a new undressed freedom.
